The Face of Bias

Project by: Ashwin Vivek, Ayden Beem, Collin Krugler, Jeff Payne, Rohan Desai-Hunt

Description

With the continuous development and advancement of AI throughout all forms of media, questions about its implications and ethics are raised, one such way is through its perception of people based on its available databases of knowledge. This project aims to explore this biased perception of people, doing so through generating a self-portrait of yourself made by Chat GPT's Dalle based off of a limited amount of imputed knowledge.
