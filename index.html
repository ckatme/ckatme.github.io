The Face of Bias

Project by: Ashwin Vivek, Ayden Beem, Collin Krugler, Jeff Payne, Rohan

Description

With the continuous development and advancement of AI throughout all forms of media, questions about it's implications and ethics are raised, one such way is through it's perception of people based on it's available databases of knowledge. This project aims to explore this biased perception of people, doing so through generating a self portrait of yourself made by Chat GPT's Dalle based off of a limited amout of inputed knowledge. The inputed information will come in the form of your answer to questions curated by us.
